Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a classic Italian dish that is not for the faint of heart. Cheese that melts in your mouth but sticks down your throat. The most natural of Sizows on the traditional lasagna shell.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna shells
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, and Italian seasoning.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
