Hamburger Recipe

Description

This classic hamburger recipe is perfect for a summer BBQ or a quick weeknight dinner. Juicy beef patties are grilled to perfection and served on toasted buns with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast hamburger buns on the grill until golden brown.
  6. Assemble burgers with your favorite toppings.
